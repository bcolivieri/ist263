Eggplant Parmesan - Angotti's Family Restaurant

In Syracuse, New York, Agnotti's Chicken Parmesan offers a traditional meal with the cozy warmth of homemade Italian food. When the platter is delivered, it has all of the classic ingredients on it: a breaded chicken cutlet, marinara sauce, and lots of melted mozzarella cheese.

After tasting, it's evident that the meal aims for the delight that one would anticipate from such a mainstay. The breading on the chicken is just right, as it just it crumples enough to expose the soft meat underneath. Although the marinara sauce is thick, it doesn't have the taste that takes a basic dish and makes it remarkable.

Even though the melted cheese looks good, it doesn't really add much to the overall richness of the dish. Based solely on the plate's merits, it earns a modest rating of 6.8, respectable but very average, leaving room for refinement and a more distinctive touch.
